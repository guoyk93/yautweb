  How to buy YAUT (Beta)


Buying YAUT is as easy as throwing money into a pood.

Send AT LEAST 5 ADA to address addr1vycaedgwweaal9vsh04e7l09p34x3u2wqq2tpm6ld4rc2jgkvh0yx, and the system will send you 2 ADA back in 10 minutes, along with all remaining ADA converted to YAUT.
